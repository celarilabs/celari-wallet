07 — Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the C-mark icon on dark backgrounds (#0A0C14 or #0F1119)
  • Maintain clear space equal to half the icon width around the mark
  • Use horizontal lockup for headers, banners, and social media
  • Use the provided color palette for all branded materials
  • Keep letter-spacing in the wordmark consistent (10px at display size)

Don't

  • Alter the 3D gradients or recolor the C-mark icon
  • Place the mark on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Stretch, rotate, or apply effects to the logo
  • Use the old hexagonal/keyhole mark (deprecated)
  • Mix brand colors with off-palette alternatives
